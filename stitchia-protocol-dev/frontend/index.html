Kernel Modules

Composable processors orchestrate signatures, ethics, ontology, legal bindings, and dashboard generation.

Generates deterministic scroll IDs and attestations using SHA-256 anchors.

Ethics Kernel

Applies governance-aligned filters and flags forbidden patterns with profile overrides.

Scroll Operations

Every scroll travels from authored markdown to validated registry entries, with braids and exports generated automatically.

01

Author & Classify

Use the Fabrica template or `./quantum add` to scaffold metadata-rich scrolls in vault/documents/.

02

Process & Validate

Run `./quantum process <scroll.md>` to notarize, flag ethics issues, and mint braid topology.

03

Seal & Publish

Validators record approvals with `./quantum seal` before artifacts are bundled, exported, and staged for releases.

04

Visualize & Export

Dashboards, braid maps, and registry exports update in frontend/ for GitHub Pages delivery.
